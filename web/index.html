Deadlight Beta 4

Deadlight is a client for the online game SneezyMUD. Please visit the Deadlight thread in the SneezyMUD forums for more detailed information, as well as bug reports and feature requests.

Deadlight may be used either as a Flash application inside of your browser, or as an Adobe AIR application installed on your computer. Currently the functionality is identical for both, but the AIR version runs a bit faster.

Click here to start Deadlight as a Flash application, or click "Install Now" below to install as an AIR application.
